## THE TWO AXES (Summary)

```
                    DOCUMENTED (+)
                         │
                         │
        ACCOUNTABLE (+) ─┼─ PROTECTED (-)
                         │
                         │
                    HIDDEN (-)
```

| Axis | Left (-100) | Right (+100) | Gameplay Meaning |
|------|-------------|--------------|------------------|
| **Documented ↔ Hidden** | Anonymous, untraceable | Public, recorded | Tracking vulnerability |
| **Accountable ↔ Protected** | Above consequences | Subject to judgment | Curse/penalty resistance |
