## THE SYSTEM'S DUNGEON ATTACK PATTERNS

### AI Combat Behavior Matrix

The System uses each dungeon's thematic attack patterns based on game state and player behavior.

#### 🎭 THE PUPPETEER (Control/Disruption AI)
**Trigger:** Player has strong board position or card advantage

| Phase | Behavior | Pattern |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Setup** | Plays String Attachment and Invisible Hand | Establishes control infrastructure |
| **Execution** | Uses Grand Theatre to disrupt player hand | Forces suboptimal plays |
| **Finisher** | Final Curtain to reset board when behind | Wipes advantages, starts fresh |

**Counter-Strategy:** Play low-frequency cards (Delta, Theta) to bypass manipulation. Save high-impact cards for after Puppeteer's control effects expire.

---

#### 🔥 THE INQUISITOR (Aggro/Reveal AI)
**Trigger:** Player is defensive or healing-heavy

| Phase | Behavior | Pattern |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Setup** | Search Warrant to scout player hand | Identifies threats |
| **Execution** | Confession Extracted to remove key cards | Pressure + resource denial |
| **Finisher** | Trial By Ordeal or Auto-Da-Fé for burst damage | Punishes healing attempts |

**Counter-Strategy:** Maintain multiple threat vectors. Don't over-invest in single cards. Use Theta frequency for unpredictability.

---

#### ⚗️ THE ALCHEMIST (Resource/Midrange AI)

| Phase | Behavior | Pattern |

## DESIGN NOTES

### The Inversion Philosophy
Every blessing has a shadow. The curse mechanic ensures no card is purely beneficial—mirroring the book's themes of shadow work and the cost of power.

### Frequency as Identity
Your frequency choice defines your playstyle:
- **Low frequencies (Delta, Theta):** Defensive, controlling, patient
- **Mid frequencies (Alpha, Beta):** Balanced, adaptive, tactical
- **High frequencies (Gamma, 432Hz):** Aggressive, transformative, risky
- **Earth frequencies (Schumann):** Stable, grounding, resource-focused

### The Seven Layers Connection
Each dungeon corresponds to layers of perception:
1. **Pedant** → Layer of Concept (naming, categorization)
2. **Golem** → Layer of Form (creation, automation)
3. **Puppeteer** → Layer of Influence (hidden forces)
4. **Inquisitor** → Layer of Truth (painful clarity)
5. **Alchemist** → Layer of Transformation (change, synthesis)
6. **Feed** → Layer of Consumption (desire, hunger)
7. **Identifier** → Layer of Self (identity, ego)
