# Card Balance Justification
## The Inversion Excursion - Core Set (21 Cards)

---

## Balance Formula Applied

**Power = (BaseStat × TierMultiplier) - (CurseSeverity × 0.7)**

| Tier | Multiplier | Rarity | Design Intent |
|------|------------|--------|---------------|
| Physical | 1.0x | Common | Reliable, straightforward, minor curses |
| Emotional | 1.2x | Uncommon | Situational power, moderate curses |
| Atomic | 1.5x | Rare | High impact, significant curses |

**Curse Severity Scale (1-20):**
- 1-5: Minor inconvenience
- 6-10: Meaningful cost
- 11-15: Significant drawback
- 16-20: Severe punishment

## THE PEDANT (Order/Control Dungeon)

### P-1: CITATION REQUIRED
**Calculated Power: 12.5 | Efficiency: 12.5**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Block 15 + Draw 1 = 16 base value (defense + card advantage)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.0x (Physical) = 16.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** +1 energy cost next card = Severity 5 (tempo loss only)
- **Final:** 16.0 - (5 × 0.7) = **12.5**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Strong for 1-cost: 15 block + draw is excellent value
- Curse delays your next play but doesn't reduce its effectiveness
- Good opening card when opponent plays first
- Weak as a finisher (curse hurts more when resources are tight)

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Clay Fist (G-1) deals more damage and has lower curse severity, but provides no card advantage. Index of Forbidden Knowledge (P-2) provides more control but costs more and has harsher curse.

---

### P-2: INDEX OF FORBIDDEN KNOWLEDGE
**Calculated Power: 9.6 | Efficiency: 4.8**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Hand reveal + card lock = 15 value (information is powerful)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.2x (Emotional) = 18.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** Discard highest cost card = Severity 12 (resource destruction)
- **Final:** 18.0 - (12 × 0.7) = **9.6**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- High variance: If opponent has no good cards, this is devastating. If they're top-decking, you discard nothing.
- The curse can be worse than the benefit if you discard your own win condition
- Requires game knowledge to use effectively
- Low efficiency (4.8) reflects situational nature

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Search Warrant (I-1) provides similar information with guaranteed chip damage and lower curse. Citation Required (P-1) provides defense instead of disruption.

---

### P-3: THE FINAL FOOTNOTE
**Calculated Power: 42.0 | Efficiency: 14.0**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Strike 25 + permanent +10 max HP = 35 value (scaling threat)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.5x (Atomic) = 52.5 calculated power
- **Curse:** Skip next draw = Severity 15 (massive tempo loss)
- **Final:** 52.5 - (15 × 0.7) = **42.0**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Highest efficiency in the set (14.0) - this card scales into late game
- Skip draw is brutal: you lose card advantage and potential answers
- Best played when you have board control and can afford the tempo loss
- Permanent HP bonus makes this attractive for control decks

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Trial By Ordeal (I-3) deals similar damage with healing but risks giving opponent cards. Rebellion Protocol (G-3) has board clear potential but requires token setup.

## THE GOLEM (Creation/Servitude Dungeon)

### G-1: CLAY FIST
**Calculated Power: 19.2 | Efficiency: 19.2**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Strike 20 (+5 combo) = 22.5 average value
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.0x (Physical) = 22.5 calculated power
- **Curse:** -1 speed next card = Severity 4 (minimal tempo)
- **Final:** 22.5 - (4 × 0.7) = **19.2**

- Simple and effective - the "vanilla" beatstick

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Animation Scroll (G-2) provides sustained value through tokens. Search Warrant (I-1) provides information. Pure damage is one-dimensional.

---

### G-2: ANIMATION SCROLL
**Calculated Power: 17.0 | Efficiency: 8.5**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** 10 HP token, 5 ATK/turn = 20 sustained value
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.2x (Emotional) = 24.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** +5 damage taken while token exists = Severity 10 (vulnerability window)
- **Final:** 24.0 - (10 × 0.7) = **17.0**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Token must survive 2 turns to break even on curse cost
- Vulnerability to Feed cards (Rebellion Protocol hard-counters)
- Creates must-answer threat that taxes opponent's removal
- Low efficiency (8.5) reflects fragility

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Philosopher's Stone (A-3) provides immediate HP without vulnerability window. Cross-Wire (PP-3) provides defense without summoning risk.

---

### G-3: REBELLION PROTOCOL
**Calculated Power: 35.2 | Efficiency: 11.73**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** 15 damage per token destroyed = 30 value (scales)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.5x (Atomic) = 45.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** Take 10 damage = Severity 14 (guaranteed self-harm)
- **Final:** 45.0 - (14 × 0.7) = **35.2**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Meta-dependent: Useless against non-token decks, devastating against Golem strategies
- Self-damage limits how aggressively you can use this
- Can be a suicide card if used at low HP
- Tech choice, not main-deck material

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Feeding Frenzy (F-3) deals damage without requiring opponent to have tokens. Trial By Ordeal (I-3) provides healing to offset self-damage.

## THE ALCHEMIST (Transformation Dungeon)

### A-1: PRIMORDIAL MUD
**Calculated Power: 4.4 | Efficiency: 4.4**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Convert 10 damage to shield = 10 value
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.0x (Physical) = 10.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** Shield decays 5 = Severity 8 (temporary benefit)
- **Final:** 10.0 - (8 × 0.7) = **4.4**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Lowest power score in core set
- Shield decay makes this a temporary fix, not a solution
- Only useful if you've already taken damage
- Synergy with cards that benefit from low HP

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Citation Required (P-1) provides guaranteed 15 block. Emet Truth (G-4, if available) provides scaling shield without decay.

---

### A-2: MERCURIAL MOOD
**Calculated Power: 8.1 | Efficiency: 4.05**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Change next frequency = 12 value (utility)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.2x (Emotional) = 14.4 calculated power
- **Curse:** 5 damage per change = Severity 9 (scaling cost)
- **Final:** 14.4 - (9 × 0.7) = **8.1**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Lowest efficiency (4.05) - this is a combo enabler, not value
- Enables resonance chains that can be powerful
- Multiple changes in one turn become expensive quickly
- Niche card for frequency-focused decks

**Why Not Strictly Better:** No other card provides frequency manipulation in core set. This is unique utility with appropriate cost.

---

### A-3: PHILOSOPHER'S STONE
**Calculated Power: 24.8 | Efficiency: 8.27**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Discard 2 → HP equal to cost = 24 average value (assumes 3-cost average)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.5x (Atomic) = 36.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** Opponent gains half as shield = Severity 16 (opponent benefit)
- **Final:** 36.0 - (16 × 0.7) = **24.8**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- High variance: Can heal 2-10 HP depending on discarded cards
- Opponent shield is significant - you're arming them while healing
- Best when opponent already has shield (wasted value)
- Alchemist identity card - conversion with cost

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Trial By Ordeal (I-3) provides healing without arming opponent. Bottomless Appetite (F-2) can heal more but costs more resources.

## THE FEED (Consumption/Mill Dungeon)

### F-1: EMPTY BOWL
**Calculated Power: 8.0 | Efficiency: 8.0**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Mill 1 + Heal 5 = 15 value (control + sustain)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.0x (Physical) = 15.0 calculated power
- **Curse:** Self-mill 1 = Severity 10 (symmetric mill)
- **Final:** 15.0 - (10 × 0.7) = **8.0**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- Symmetric mill - you're thinning both decks
- Good against decks that rely on specific cards in discard
- Heal 5 is minor but adds up
- Mill strategy enabler

**Why Not Strictly Better:** String Attachment (PP-1) disrupts without self-mill. Search Warrant (I-1) provides information with damage.

---

### F-2: BOTTOMLESS APPETITE
**Calculated Power: 16.2 | Efficiency: 8.1**

**Balance Justification:**
- **Base:** Discard hand → 8 HP per card = 28.8 average (assumes 3 cards)
- **Tier Multiplier:** 1.2x (Emotional) = 28.8 calculated power
- **Curse:** No draw next turn = Severity 18 (massive card disadvantage)
- **Final:** 28.8 - (18 × 0.7) = **16.2**

**Trade-off Analysis:**
- High ceiling: Can heal 24+ HP with full hand
- No draw next turn is devastating - you're top-decking
- Best as finisher when you have lethal next turn
- Emergency button, not strategy

**Why Not Strictly Better:** Philosopher's Stone (A-3) provides healing without hand destruction. Trial By Ordeal (I-3) provides healing without the draw skip.

---

- Self-damage is significant cost - can kill yourself
