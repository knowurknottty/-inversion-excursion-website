## META PREDICTIONS

### Tier 1 Deck Archetypes (Predicted)

1. **Inquisitor Burn** (I-2, I-3, I-4, A-5)
   - Fast damage, purge opponent resources
   - Weakness: Golem token swarm

2. **Golem Value** (G-2, G-3, G-4, G-5)
   - Token generation, scaling threats
   - Weakness: Feed mill, Inquisitor purge

3. **Puppeteer Control** (PP-2, PP-3, PP-4, PP-5)
   - Hand disruption, board control
   - Weakness: Aggro burn, Identifier tech

4. **Alchemist Combo** (A-2, A-3, A-4, A-5)
   - Resource conversion, late-game swing
   - Weakness: Early pressure, Feed mill

### Counter-Cycle

```
Inquisitor (Aggro) → Feed (Mill) → Alchemist (Combo) → 
Golem (Tokens) → Puppeteer (Control) → Inquisitor (Aggro)
```

**Rock-Paper-Scissors:**
- Aggro beats Combo (too slow)
- Mill beats Tokens (removes board)
- Combo beats Control (out-values)
- Tokens beats Aggro (chump block)
- Control beats Mill (hand refill)

## BALANCE ADJUSTMENT RECOMMENDATIONS

### Potential Nerfs

| Card | Issue | Suggested Change |
|------|-------|------------------|
| A-5 Great Work | Too swingy, can OTK | Reduce shield conversion to 75% |
| I-5 Absolute Truth | Draw skip too harsh | Reduce to 1 turn no draw |
| F-5 Mouth Never Closes | Permanent discard oppressive | Add "until you play 5 cards" limit |

### Potential Buffs

| Card | Issue | Suggested Change |
|------|-------|------------------|
| ID-1 Label Gun | Too weak for cost | Add "draw 1 card" effect |
| A-1 Primordial Mud | Shield decay too fast | Reduce decay to 3 |
| PP-1 String Attachment | Symmetric reveal too costly | Remove reveal curse |

### Tier Adjustments for Release

- Move **I-3 Trial By Ordeal** to Uncommon (too swingy for Rare slot)
- Move **G-3 Rebellion Protocol** to Epic (too powerful for Rare)
- Move **H-4 Anonymous Accuser** to Rare (50% self-curse balances power)
