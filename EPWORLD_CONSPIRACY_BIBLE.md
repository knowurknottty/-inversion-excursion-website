# EPWORLD: CONSPIRACY SWARM
## Complete Story Bible

---

# OVERVIEW

**Genre:** Conspiracy Thriller / Investigative RPG  
**Tone:** Paranoid, methodical, unsettling  
**Core Premise:** The player is a digital archivist who stumbles upon encrypted fragments of a larger truth—one that powerful people have killed to keep hidden. The game world is a simulation of interconnected databases, dark web forums, classified document leaks, and surveillance footage.

**The Four Pillars:**
1. **The Noose Paradox** - Forensic inconsistencies in a high-profile death
2. **The Maxwell Switch** - Identity substitution through biometric manipulation
3. **The Orange Connection** - Flight logs linking the powerful to the island
4. **The Continuity** - Evidence that operations never stopped

*Theme: The first crack in the facade*

### Quest 1: "The Dead Man's Switch"
**Location:** Player's terminal / Dark web entry node  
**Unlocks:** Basic investigation tools

**Setup:**  
Player receives an automated data dump from a dead man's switch—an encrypted archive that was programmed to release if its owner didn't check in for 30 days. The owner was a forensic technician who worked at the medical examiner's office.

**Objective:**  
Decrypt and analyze the first document: a preliminary autopsy report with handwritten annotations.

**Key Discoveries:**
- Official report lists cause as "suicide by hanging"
- Handwritten notes question: "Hyoid bone intact? Check again."
- Timestamp anomaly: report filed 4 hours before body officially examined
- Signature mismatch: two different handwriting styles on the same document

**Cliffhanger:**  
As the player finishes decrypting, their terminal receives a single packet ping—a tracking beacon that reveals someone knows they're looking.

### Quest 2: "Broken Bones"
**Location:** Medical examiner database / Anatomy archives  
**Prerequisite:** Quest 1  
**Unlocks:** Forensic analysis module

**Setup:**  
The player must verify the anatomical impossibility mentioned in the report. They access forensic textbooks, case studies, and medical literature to understand what a genuine hanging death should look like.

**Objective:**  
Compile evidence that the official cause contradicts established forensic science.

**Key Discoveries:**
- In hanging deaths, the hyoid bone fractures in approximately 90% of cases
- The victim's age (66) increases probability of fracture to 95%+
- Three cervical vertebrae should show trauma; official report notes "no significant trauma"
- Comparison with genuine hanging suicides in same facility shows consistent fracture patterns
- The bedsheet (alleged ligature) was 6 feet long; cell ceiling was 8.5 feet high; victim was 6 feet tall—mathematically impossible to generate sufficient force

**Unlockable Evidence:**
- Forensic expert interviews (audio files)
- 3D cell reconstruction showing physical impossibility
- Comparison case files from same facility

**Cliffhanger:**  
Player receives a message: "DELETE THIS SEARCH. YOU DON'T KNOW WHAT YOU'RE DIGGING UP." IP traces to a government server.

- The cells on either side of the victim were empty that night—unusual for a high-security unit

# CONCLUSION

This story bible provides a complete narrative structure for EPWORLD's conspiracy layer. The design emphasizes:

1. **Progressive Revelation** - Truth unfolds in layers, each more disturbing than the last
2. **Player Agency** - Choices matter, with five distinct endings plus secret variant
3. **Moral Complexity** - No pure heroes or villains; everyone compromised, everyone choosing
4. **Real-World Anchoring** - Based on actual evidence, inconsistencies, and unanswered questions
5. **Systemic Critique** - The conspiracy is not aberration but evolution of existing power structures

The narrative asks the ultimate question: 

**"If the truth is too dangerous to know, do you still want to know it?"**

And follows up with:

**"If you know it, what are you willing to sacrifice to tell it?"**

The player's journey is from curiosity to knowledge to choice to consequence. The world they inhabit is our world, slightly extrapolated. The power they confront is real power, as it exists today. The victory, if it comes, is partial and costly—because that's what victory against power actually looks like.
