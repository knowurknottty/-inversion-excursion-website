Every phrase in this grimoire is constructed upon five pillars:

1. **Intent** - The crystallized purpose behind the words
2. **Resonance** - The vibrational match to desired outcomes
3. **Anchoring** - The connection to ancient power through etymology
4. **Delivery** - The energetic signature of how words are spoken
5. **Timing** - The contextual appropriateness of deployment

| Aspect | Shadow (Stealth) | Light (Overt) |

| Audience | Unaware subjects | Conscious participants |
