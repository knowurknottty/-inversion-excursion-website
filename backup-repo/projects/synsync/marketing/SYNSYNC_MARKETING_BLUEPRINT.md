## THE SCIENCE (Simplified)

### What is Brainwave Entrainment?

Your brain produces electrical pulses at different frequencies:
- **Beta (13-30 Hz)** — Active thinking, problem-solving
- **Alpha (8-13 Hz)** — Relaxed awareness, creativity
- **Theta (4-8 Hz)** — Deep meditation, memory access
- **Delta (0.5-4 Hz)** — Deep sleep, healing

**The problem:** Modern life keeps us stuck in high-beta (stress, anxiety, overthinking).

**The solution:** Binaural beats, isochronic tones, and monaural beats that gently guide your brain to the frequency you need.
