## 📋 BOOKING TIPS

### Where to Book:
- **Booking.com** - Best for free cancellation options
- **Hotels.com** - Rewards program (stay 10 nights, get 1 free)
- **Expedia** - Package deals with flights
- **Direct hotel websites** - Often match prices + perks

### Money-Saving Tips:
1. Book 30+ days in advance for best rates
2. Look for non-refundable rates if plans are firm (10-15% savings)
3. Consider week-long stays for extended stay discounts
4. Check Florida resident rates if applicable
5. Mid-week check-ins (Tuesday/Wednesday) often cheaper

### March 2026 Considerations:
- Spring break season begins mid-March
- Book ASAP for best availability under $150
- Prices may fluctuate; set price alerts on booking sites
