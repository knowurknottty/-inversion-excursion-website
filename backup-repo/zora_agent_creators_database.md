### 2.2 Content Coin Model
- Every post becomes a fixed-supply ERC-20 token (1 billion supply)
- Creator receives 1% (10 million tokens) immediately upon posting
- Trading fees (~3% round-trip) split:
  - 1% to content creator (in ZORA tokens)
  - 1% to protocol
  - 1% to liquidity providers

### 2.3 Creator Coin Model
- Each user gets a Creator Coin upon registration
- Supply: 1 billion tokens
- Distribution:
  - 50% immediately tradable
  - 50% (500M) unlocks linearly over 5 years based on trading activity
- Revenue streams:
  - Transaction fees from content coin trades
  - Trading/unlocking revenue from Creator Coin itself
