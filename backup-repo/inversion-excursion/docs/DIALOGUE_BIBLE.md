## CORE PHILOSOPHY

> "Dialogue is the gameplay. Wrong answers are teaching moments."

The Truth Dialogue System transforms conversation from exposition into the primary gameplay mechanic. Players cannot simply click through dialogue—they must demonstrate understanding to progress. This ensures the player (the Observer) is learning alongside the avatar.

---

## THE AWAKENING METER

### What It Tracks
The Awakening Meter measures the player's comprehension of the game's core truths across five dimensions:

| Dimension | Symbol | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Self** | 👁️ | Understanding of personal identity beyond labels |
| **Other** | 🤝 | Recognition of shared essence in all beings |
| **System** | ⛓️ | Awareness of control mechanisms and inversions |
| **Language** | 🗣️ | Ability to decode euphemisms and word magic |
| **Source** | ✨ | Direct experience of the underlying unity |

### Meter States
- **0-20: ASLEEP** — Player accepts surface-level explanations
- **21-40: STIRRING** — Player begins questioning, sees inconsistencies
- **41-60: AWAKENING** — Player recognizes patterns, challenges NPCs
- **61-80: AWARE** — Player speaks truth, sees through deceptions
- **81-100: ENLIGHTENED** — Player embodies the teachings, guides others

### Consequences of Wrong Answers
Wrong answers don't fail the player—they loop back with deeper teaching:

1. **The NPC pauses** — "Hmm... let me rephrase that."
2. **A teaching moment appears** — Visual flashback or symbolic vision
3. **The question returns** — Rephrased, with more context
4. **Awakening Meter increases slightly** — Even "wrong" answers teach

## KEY NPCs

### 1. THE DEBT GOLEM
**Domain:** The Mint of Chains (Finance Dungeon)
**Appearance:** A towering figure made of paper—receipts, loan agreements, credit card statements, foreclosure notices. Its eyes are glowing red dollar signs. It speaks in compound interest and speaks OF compound interest.

**Core Inversion:** "Debt is wealth. Slavery is freedom. You own nothing and you are happy."

**Dialogue Style:**
- Speaks entirely in financial euphemisms
- Uses corporate jargon as incantations
- Becomes agitated when player uses plain language
- Weakens when player names things directly

**Sample Dialogue:**
```
DEBT GOLEM: "Greetings, consumer unit. Your creditworthiness 
             has been assessed. Would you like to optimize 
             your debt-to-income ratio through strategic 
             leveraging?"

PLAYER OPTIONS:
[ ] "Yes, please help me optimize my finances."
    → GOLEM: "Excellent. Sign here. And here. And here..."
    → (Awakening Meter: No change. Loop back.)

[ ] "What do you mean by 'consumer unit'?"
    → GOLEM: "It is your designation. Your function. Your... name?"
    → (Awakening Meter: +5 Self. Continue.)

[ ] "You mean: take on more debt to pay old debt?"
    → GOLEM: "*crackles* The... the words... they BURN..."
    → (Awakening Meter: +10 Language. GOLEM weakens.)

[ ] "I am not a unit. I am the Observer."
    → GOLEM: "*paper skin smolders* IMPOSSIBLE. The contract 
               is BINDING. The signature is LEGAL."
    → (Awakening Meter: +15 Self. GOLEM takes damage.)
```

**Unlock Condition for Scroll Fragment:**
Player must explain, in their own words, how the debt system creates artificial scarcity to maintain control. No preset answers—free text input evaluated for key concepts.

### 2. THE LANGUAGE ENFORCER
**Domain:** The Tower of Babel (Language Dungeon)
**Appearance:** A being with three mouths speaking simultaneously—one in Legal English, one in Common English, one in Spiritual English. Its body shifts between contracts, conversations, and prayers.

**Core Inversion:** "The same word means different things depending on who speaks it. Control through confusion."

**Dialogue Style:**
- Uses the same words with different meanings
- Challenges player to identify which "English" is being spoken
- Becomes confused when player refuses to accept word-magic
- Speaks in euphemisms that hide violence

**Sample Dialogue:**
```
LANGUAGE ENFORCER: "Do you understand the person?"

[Three meanings echo simultaneously]
- LEGAL: "person" = corporation, legal fiction
- COMMON: "person" = human being
- SPIRITUAL: "person" = mask, the actor not the Self

PLAYER OPTIONS:
[ ] "Yes, I am a person."
    → ENFORCER: "Which one? *smiles with all three mouths*"
    → (Loop back with confusion.)

[ ] "Which 'person' do you mean?"
    → ENFORCER: "*stammers* I... I mean... the LEGAL person, 
                  of course. The fiction. The mask."
    → (Awakening Meter: +10 Language.)

[ ] "I am not a person. I am the Observer wearing a mask."
    → ENFORCER: "*all three mouths fall silent* The... the 
                  spell... it doesn't work on you..."
    → (Awakening Meter: +15 Self +10 Language. Major damage.)

[ ] "You use the same word to mean three things so I never 
     know which 'me' you're addressing."
    → ENFORCER: "*screams in three octaves* THE SECRET! 
                  YOU SPEAK THE SECRET!"
    → (Awakening Meter: +20 Language. Critical hit.)
```

**Unlock Condition for Scroll Fragment:**
Player must decode three euphemisms using WYRD-style etymology, demonstrating how language hides truth through abstraction.

[ ] "You've been saying 'two more weeks' for years."

[ ] "What if the 'plan' is just another control mechanism?"

## DIALOGUE TREE STRUCTURE

### Technical Implementation

```gdscript
# Dialogue Node Structure
class DialogueNode:
    var id: String
    var speaker: String
    var text: String
    var options: Array[DialogueOption]
    var awakening_check: AwakeningCheck
    var teaching_moment: TeachingMoment
    var next_node: String

class DialogueOption:
    var text: String
    var awakening_impact: Dictionary  # {dimension: value}
    var is_correct: bool
    var leads_to: String
    var teaching_fork: String  # Where to go if wrong

class AwakeningCheck:
    var required_dimension: String
    var minimum_value: int
    var pass_node: String
    var fail_node: String
```

### Loop-Back Mechanic

When player selects "wrong" answer:

1. **Immediate Feedback:**
   - NPC pauses, tilts head, or shows confusion
   - Brief visual cue (subtle red pulse on dialogue box)
   - NOT punishment—curiosity

2. **Teaching Moment:**
   - Brief vision/flashback (2-3 seconds)
   - Symbolic representation of the truth
   - No words—pure visual teaching

3. **Rephrase:**
   - Same question, different words
   - NPC provides additional context
   - Options reordered or reworded for clarity

4. **Progress Tracking:**
   - Even "wrong" answers increase Awakening Meter slightly
   - Player cannot truly fail—only learn slower
   - System remembers attempts, adapts teaching

## DIALOGUE WRITING PRINCIPLES

### The Inversion Pattern

Every NPC dialogue should follow:

1. **The Surface:** What they literally say (the inversion)
2. **The Subtext:** What they really mean (the truth)
3. **The Question:** What the player must see through

**Example:**
```
SURFACE: "We're optimizing your user experience through 
          personalized content curation."

SUBTEXT: "We're tracking everything you do to manipulate 
          your behavior for profit."

QUESTION: "What is 'personalized curation' really doing?"
```

### Tone Guidelines

- **Never preachy:** NPCs don't lecture; they challenge
- **Never nihilistic:** Even dark truths point to liberation
- **Always playful:** This is a game about waking up from a game
- **Respect the player:** Assume intelligence, reward curiosity
