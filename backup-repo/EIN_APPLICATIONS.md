## 📝 EIN APPLICATION PROCESS

### Method 1: Online (IRS.gov) - FREE
**URL:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online

**Steps:**
1. Form LLC first (state filing)
2. Go to IRS EIN Assistant
3. Complete online application
4. Receive EIN immediately

**Hours:** Monday-Friday, 7am-10pm EST

### Method 2: Mail/Fax - FREE
**Form:** SS-4
**Time:** 4-6 weeks

### Method 3: Third Party - $50-200
**Services:** LegalZoom, Incfile, Northwest Registered Agent

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES

### Tax Implications
- LLC = Pass-through taxation (you report on personal return)
- Consider S-Corp election if profitable ($50k+)
- Track all expenses from day one

### Liability Protection
- LLC separates personal/business assets
- Maintain corporate formalities (meetings, records)
- Don't commingle funds

### Crypto Considerations
- Sheckle DAO LLC provides legal wrapper
