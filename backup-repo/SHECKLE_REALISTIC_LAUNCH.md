## What Pump.fun Can and Can't Do

### Pump.fun Limitations
- **No custom smart contracts** — It's a token factory, not a dev platform
- **No self-destruct mechanisms** — Can't code automatic rebalancing
- **No on-chain governance** — Can't vote to change parameters
- **Fixed supply** — Whatever you mint at launch is what exists

### What You CAN Do
- **Burn your own tokens** — Send dev allocation to burn address
- **Lock liquidity** — Use a third-party locker (UNCX, Mudra)
- **Renounce ownership** — Remove mint authority (can't create more)
- **Transparent distribution** — Show exactly where tokens went
