# Excluded Papers - Brainwave Entrainment
## Papers rejected from SynSync evidence base

---

## REJECTION CRITERIA

Papers excluded for:
- No empirical validation
- Predatory journal
- Insufficient methodology
- Metaphysical/esoteric claims
- Conference proceedings without peer review

---

## EXCLUDED PAPERS

### 1. "Music of the Sphere" (First, 2003)
**DOI:** 10.1162/096112104322750755  
**Reason:** 🜏 SYMBOLIC - No empirical basis  
**Claim:** Earth as giant bell, asymptotic harmonics affect brainwaves  
**Issue:** Metaphysical framework without measurable outcomes

**Verdict:** Exclude from clinical claims. May reference in "history of sound healing" context only.

## LESSONS

**What this tells us:**
- Brainwave entrainment field has noise
- Must distinguish clinical from speculative
- Conference proceedings ≠ peer-reviewed evidence
- Esoteric claims dilute legitimate science

**SynSync policy:**
- Only cite papers with DOI from recognized journals
- Exclude metaphysical claims
- Flag experimental findings as 🔬 EXPERIMENTAL
- Never claim what isn't proven
