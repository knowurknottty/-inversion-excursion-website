# SAMPLE FIRST SESSION AGENDA
## Frequency Warrior Cell — Initial Gathering

**Total Duration:** 2 hours (allow extra time for first session)
**Location:** [To be determined by group]
**Materials needed:** Timer, printed Four Agreements, notebook for each person

---

## PART 1: ARRIVAL & GROUNDING (15 min)

**0:00–0:05 | Silent Arrival**
- Enter quietly
- Find your seat
- No small talk yet—just settle

**0:05–0:15 | Opening Check-In Round**
Each person answers (facilitator goes last):
1. Name and preferred pronouns
2. One word describing how you're arriving (emotionally, physically)
3. What specifically drew you to this invitation?

*Facilitator notes: Keep this moving. 2-3 minutes per person max. No responses yet—just listening.*

---

## PART 2: ESTABLISHING THE CONTAINER (35 min)

**0:15–0:25 | Reading the Four Agreements**
Facilitator reads each agreement aloud slowly. Pause after each for questions.

1. **Confidentiality** — What is shared here stays here
2. **No Advice-Giving** — We witness, we don't fix
3. **Equal Airtime** — No one dominates, no one disappears
4. **I-Statements Only** — Speak from your experience, not general truths

*Ask: "Does anyone need clarification? Any concerns?"*

**0:25–0:35 | Safety Agreement Review**
Review the Safety Agreement Template together:
- Confidentiality and its limits
- What to do if someone becomes activated
- Scope of practice (what this is NOT)

*Everyone should understand: This is not therapy, not crisis support.*

**0:35–0:50 | Logistics Decisions (Consent-Based)**
Facilitator proposes; group consents (no objections = consent):

## PART 3: CHOOSING OUR PRACTICE (15 min)

**0:50–1:05 | Practice Selection**

Options to consider (discuss, then consent):
- **Option A:** 20-minute silent meditation (sit, breathe, return to sensation)
- **Option B:** Guided somatic grounding (10 min body scan + 10 min silence)
- **Option C:** Writing prompt (10 min free write + sharing)
- **Option D:** [Group proposes alternative]

*Criteria: Simple, accessible to all, leaves energy for truth-telling*

**Decision:** _________________________

---

## PART 4: FIRST PRACTICE (30 min)

**1:05–1:35 | Do the Practice Together**

Facilitator:
- Sets timer
- Gives minimal instruction
- Participates fully (doesn't "perform" facilitation)

Everyone:
- Actually do the practice (don't observe)
- Let yourself be affected by it
- Notice what arises without judgment

---

## PART 5: FIRST TRUTH-TELLING (30 min)

**1:35–1:40 | Transition**
- One minute of silence
- Facilitator reminds: "No advice. Just witness."

**1:40–2:05 | Truth-Telling Round**

**Prompt:** "Share something true that you don't usually say in groups. Start small if you need to. You're not required to perform vulnerability. Just don't perform normalcy."

*Facilitator: Set timer for 4 minutes per person. Signal at 1 minute remaining. When someone finishes, the group responds: "Thank you for sharing." One breath together before the next person.*

## PART 6: CLOSING (15 min)

**2:05–2:15 | Check-Out Round**
Each person answers:
1. One word for how you're leaving
2. Anything you need to say before we close?

- Option A: Hand on heart, bow to the center

- Option C: Each person says one word of completion

## POST-SESSION (Facilitator Only)

**Notes for next time:**
- What worked?
- What felt awkward?
- Any concerns to watch?
- Any logistics to adjust?

**Send to group within 24 hours:**
- Confirmation of next session details
- Any decisions that need documentation
- Gratitude for showing up

---

*Remember: The first session sets the tone. Don't rush. But don't let it drift. Hold the structure—it holds the group.*
