## DOMAIN CARD 1: Functional Medicine / Biomarker Diagnostics

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **EPISTEMIC BASIS** | Empirical (with significant ⚠️ SPECULATIVE zones) |
| **LIVE SIGNAL RATING** | 6/10 |
| **BLEACHING VECTOR** | Commercialization diluted mechanistic rigor into "personalized wellness" marketing; direct-to-consumer testing bypasses clinical interpretation; supplement industry co-opted biomarker language to sell unvalidated products |
| **KNOWN CRITIQUES** | 1) Reference ranges often population-based, not individually optimized; 2) Correlation ≠ causation for many biomarker-disease links; 3) Over-testing generates false positives; 4) Lifestyle interventions show effect sizes smaller than claimed; 5) Functional medicine "root cause" framework sometimes ignores proven treatments in favor of unproven alternatives |
| **CROSS-VALIDATION MECHANISM** | Biomarker findings must predict clinical outcomes in RCTs; metabolomics should replicate across labs; genetic associations require GWAS validation; gut microbiome claims must show mechanistic pathways (not just correlation) |
| **MISUSE RISK** | Patient abandonment of proven treatments for "natural" alternatives based on biomarker interpretation; financial exploitation via expensive testing with no clinical utility; diagnostic delay in serious conditions |

**Key Distinction:** ✅ ESTABLISHED — Blood glucose, lipids, thyroid panels, basic metabolic markers
**⚠️ SPECULATIVE —** Organic acids, comprehensive stool analysis, food sensitivity IgG, adrenal fatigue (HPA axis dysregulation not recognized in endocrinology)
**🜏 SYMBOLIC —** "Toxic burden," "cellular energy," "detoxification pathways" (metaphorical language without measurable endpoints)

## DOMAIN CARD 2: Biofield / Resonance Modeling (Plasma Analogies)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **EPISTEMIC BASIS** | 🜏 SYMBOLIC (entire domain) |
| **LIVE SIGNAL RATING** | 2/10 |
| **BLEACHING VECTOR** | New Age commercialization stripped the rigorous phenomenology from Reich's orgone and Burr's L-field; physics terminology (field, resonance, plasma, frequency) borrowed without mathematical formalism; measurable biological effects (if any) conflated with claimed field properties |
| **KNOWN CRITIQUES** | 1) No reproducible biofield detection independent of known electromagnetic fields; 2) Plasma analogies are metaphorical, not mathematical; 3) "Resonance" used without specifying coupled oscillators; 4) No predictive model that differs from placebo/relaxation effects; 5) Measurement devices (GDV, Bio-Well) lack peer validation |
| **CROSS-VALIDATION MECHANISM** | Biofield claims must predict measurable biological outcomes better than known mechanisms; field detectors must distinguish biofield from environmental EMF; therapeutic effects must replicate in double-blind RCTs against sham |
| **MISUSE RISK** | Delay of proven medical treatment; financial exploitation; false diagnostic authority from "scans" showing "field imbalances" |

**Critical Constraint:** This entire domain operates as 🜏 SYMBOLIC. The language of "fields" and "resonance" is metaphorical, not physical. The valid use is as a **cognitive technology for somatic awareness**, not as a physical model.

**Valid Application:** Using "field" language to guide attention to interoceptive states; "resonance" as metaphor for interpersonal attunement.
**Invalid Application:** Claiming measurable physical fields; using "frequency" without Hz specification; treating symbolic maps as causal mechanisms.

**🜏 SYMBOLIC —** "Cognitive architecture" as metaphor for self-understanding (valid as introspective tool, not as literal neural wiring diagram)

**Key Distinction:** 🜏 SYMBOLIC — Astrology as **cognitive technology for timing, self-reflection, and narrative construction**

**Valid Application:** Using planetary cycles as metaphor for developmental timing (Saturn return as symbolic marker, not causal force); chart as prompt for self-inquiry; archetypal language (Mars = assertion, Venus = values) as vocabulary for psychological observation.
**Invalid Application:** Claiming planetary causation; predicting specific events; using astrology for medical diagnosis; treating chart as deterministic.

**Interpretive Framework for Intellectual Honesty:**
- Tag all astrological claims as 🜏 SYMBOLIC

- Explicitly bracket literal truth claims
- Acknowledge Carlson study and precession problem directly
- Treat chart as **hermeneutic tool** (interpretive framework), not **predictive model**
