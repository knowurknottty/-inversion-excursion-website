# SynSync Regulatory Theater Marketing Campaign
## "The Things We're Not Allowed To Tell You"

**Campaign Concept:** Use FDA/FTC restrictions as a marketing weapon by educating consumers about what wellness apps CANNOT legally claim—then letting user testimony speak the forbidden truth.

**Core Insight:** The regulatory system prohibits honest small businesses from making true claims while allowing pharmaceutical giants to market products later proven ineffective. This injustice is your marketing angle.

---

## THE FORBIDDEN CLAIMS LIST

These are the claims SynSync CANNOT legally make. They become your content pillars:

### Tier 1: Absolute Prohibitions (Never say these)
- "Cures anxiety"
- "Treats ADHD"
- "Stops panic attacks"
- "Cures insomnia"
- "Medical-grade treatment"
- "FDA-approved"

### Tier 2: Implied Prohibitions (Careful framing needed)
- "Works in 2 minutes" → "Users report effects in as little as 2 minutes"
- "100% effective" → "100% of surveyed users reported efficacy"
- "Zero side effects" → "Users report no adverse effects"
- "Better than medication" → "Users compare their experience to medication"

### Tier 3: Comparative Prohibitions (Use user voice)
- "More effective than Xanax" → User says: "I switched from Xanax to this"
- "Beats meditation apps" → User says: "This worked when Calm didn't"
- "Pharma alternative" → User says: "I stopped taking [medication] after trying this"
