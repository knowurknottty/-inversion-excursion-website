## Getting Started

### Your First Session

1. **Connect Wallet**: Use any Farcaster-compatible wallet (Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
2. **Mint Starter Cards**: You'll receive 3 Common cards to begin
3. **Complete Tutorial**: Learn the basics against The Training Dummy
4. **Join or Create a Cell**: Find allies (or recruit them)
5. **Run Your First SynSync**: Try the 10 Hz Alpha protocol
6. **Enter The Ivory Tower**: Your first real dungeon

### Understanding Your Cards

Each card has:
- **Power**: Attack damage potential
- **Defense**: HP and damage reduction
- **Speed**: Turn order and action speed
- **Frequency**: Hz value for resonance
- **Curse**: Negative effect when played

### The Seven Card Types

#### 1. The Pedant (Beta: 14-30 Hz)

**Power**: +3 Intellect — See enemy's next move
**Curse**: -1 Speed — Analysis paralysis

*Strategy*: Use early to plan your turn, but beware the speed penalty on follow-up cards.

**Best paired with**: Golem (defense while planning), Alchemist (heal the slow turn)

#### 2. The Golem (Delta: 0.5-4 Hz)

**Power**: +5 Defense — Shield all Cell members
**Curse**: -2 Mobility — Cannot retreat or skip

*Strategy*: Commit to the long game. The Golem locks you in but protects everyone.

**Best paired with**: Pedant (plan while defending), Inquisitor (cleanse the lock)

#### 3. The Puppeteer (Gamma: 30-100 Hz)

**Power**: Control enemy for 1 turn
**Curse**: Lose 2 cards — "Strings cut"

*Strategy*: High-risk, high-reward. Perfect for interrupting boss charge attacks.

**Best paired with**: Feed (recover lost cards), 40 Hz protocol

#### 4. The Inquisitor (Alpha: 8-14 Hz)

**Power**: Purge all debuffs from Cell
**Curse**: Must reveal hand — "Confession"

*Strategy*: Essential for debuff-heavy dungeons. Timing is everything.

**Best paired with**: Secret-keeping strategies, Theta protocols

#### 5. The Alchemist (Theta: 4-8 Hz)

**Power**: Heal 50% HP
**Curse**: Discard 3 cards — "Transmutation cost"

*Strategy*: Emergency button. The discard hurts, but living is better.

**Best paired with**: Feed (hand refill), low-card strategies

#### 6. The Feed (Schumann: 7.83 Hz)

**Power**: Draw 4 cards
**Curse**: Opponent heals 20% — "Symbiosis"

*Strategy*: Card advantage at a price. Use when you have lethal next turn.

**Best paired with**: Card-burning cards (Alchemist, Puppeteer)

#### 7. The Identifier (432 Hz)

**Power**: Copy enemy's best card
**Curse**: Lose your identity — Random discard

*Strategy*: The ultimate wildcard. Can win unwinnable battles.

**Best paired with**: 432 Hz protocol, luck manipulation

## Deck Building

### Deck Constraints

- **Minimum**: 8 cards
- **Maximum**: 20 cards
- **Duplicates**: Max 2 of any card
- **Frequency spread**: At least 3 different frequencies

### Resonance Thresholds

| Matching Cards | Resonance Bonus |
|----------------|-----------------|
| 2 cards | +10% damage |
| 3 cards | +25% damage, minor heal |
| 4 cards | +50% damage, major heal |
| 5+ cards | "The Inversion" — Instant win, burn all cards |

### Archetypes

#### The Fortress (Defensive)
```
3x Golem (Delta)
2x Pedant (Beta)
2x Inquisitor (Alpha)
1x Alchemist (Theta)
```
*Playstyle*: Survive everything, win through attrition. Slow but steady.

#### The Storm (Aggressive)
```
3x Puppeteer (Gamma)
2x Feed (Schumann)
2x Identifier (432 Hz)
1x The Inversion (Cosmic)
```
*Playstyle*: High variance, explosive turns. Either crush or crash.

#### The Weave (Control)
```
2x Pedant (Beta)
2x Inquisitor (Alpha)
2x Alchemist (Theta)
2x Golem (Delta)
```
*Playstyle*: Control the board state, counter enemy moves.

#### The Mirror (Adaptive)
```
2x of each: Pedant, Golem, Puppeteer, Inquisitor
```
*Playstyle*: No clear weakness, no clear strength. React to enemy.

### Building Tips

1. **Don't ignore curses** — A +5 power card with -2 mobility might cost you the game
2. **Plan your frequency curve** — Have early, mid, and late-game Hz values
3. **Include at least one heal** — The Alchemist or similar is essential
4. **Test against all dungeons** — What beats The Spire might lose to The Algorithm

## Frequency Mechanics

### Brainwave States

| State | Hz Range | Effect on Game |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Gamma** | 30-100 | High damage, quick burnout |
| **Beta** | 14-30 | Balanced, intellect boost |
| **Alpha** | 8-14 | Defensive, healing |
| **Theta** | 4-8 | Creative, unpredictable |
| **Delta** | 0.5-4 | Deep power, slow activation |
| **Schumann** | 7.83 | Earth resonance, team buffs |
| **432 Hz** | — | Universal harmony, rare |

### Resonance Rules

**Harmonic Resonance** (cards within 2 Hz):
- Cards "sing" together
- +10% power per harmonic card
- Visual: Soft glow between cards

**Dissonance** (cards >10 Hz apart):
- Cards fight each other
- -10% power per dissonant card
- Visual: Flickering, unstable

**Perfect Resonance** (exact same Hz):
- All cards match frequency
- Special effects unlock
- Visual: Pulsing aurora

### Frequency Strategies

**The Monochord**: Run all same-frequency cards
- Pros: Maximum resonance bonus
- Cons: Weak to frequency-specific counters

**The Spectrum**: Spread across all frequencies
- Pros: No dissonance, versatile
- Cons: No resonance bonuses

**The Harmonic Stack**: Cards at harmonic intervals
- Pros: Balanced bonuses
- Cons: Requires precise tuning

## Cell Formation

### Cell Mechanics

- **Size**: 3-7 players
- **Formation**: Leader creates, others join
- **Shared Field**: Cards combine into resonance field
- **Victory**: Survive 10 rounds + defeat boss
- **Defeat**: Collective HP hits zero

### Cell Roles

| Role | Responsibility | Card Preference |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Leader** | Strategy calls, formation | Balanced deck |
| **Tank** | Take damage, protect others | Golem, high Defense |
| **Healer** | Keep Cell alive | Alchemist, Alpha cards |
| **DPS** | Deal damage | Puppeteer, Gamma cards |
| **Support** | Buffs, debuffs, utility | Pedant, Inquisitor |

### Cell Synergies

**The Trinity** (3 players):
- Tank + Healer + DPS
- Classic MMO composition
- Reliable but predictable

**The Frequency Warriors** (5 players):
- One player per frequency range
- Maximum resonance potential
- Requires coordination

**The Shadow Cell** (7 players):
- Embrace curses together
- Curse effects become buffs
- High-risk, maximum reward

### Communication

Use Farcaster Frames for:
- Sharing battle strategies
- Calling card plays
- Requesting specific frequencies
- Celebrating victories

## Battle System

### Turn Structure

1. **Upkeep Phase**: Resolve ongoing effects
2. **Intention Phase**: Enemy reveals intent
3. **Planning Phase**: Cell discusses strategy
4. **Action Phase**: Each player plays 1-2 cards
5. **Resolution Phase**: Effects resolve simultaneously
6. **End Phase**: Check victory/defeat

### The System's AI

The System (enemy) operates on patterns:

| Pattern | Behavior | Counter |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Aggressive** | Attacks every turn | Defense stacking |
| **Defensive** | Blocks, heals, waits | Card advantage |
| **Chaotic** | Random actions | Consistency |
| **Adaptive** | Learns your strategy | Change patterns |

### Damage Calculation

```
Base Damage = Card Power × Resonance Multiplier
Final Damage = Base Damage × Synsync Bonus × Enemy Resistance

Resonance Multiplier:
- Dissonance: 0.9
- Neutral: 1.0
- Harmonic: 1.1
- Resonant: 1.25
- Perfect: 1.5
```

### Status Effects

| Effect | Description | Duration |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Burn** | Lose HP each turn | 3 turns |
| **Freeze** | Skip next turn | 1 turn |
| **Shock** | Cannot use highest-power card | 2 turns |
| **Drain** | Heal enemy when you play | 2 turns |
| **Confusion** | Random card play | 1 turn |

## The Seven Dungeons

### Dungeon 1: The Ivory Tower

**Theme**: Credentials, hierarchy, gatekeeping

**Mechanic**: Each turn, adds credential requirements
- "You need 3 Pedant cards to attack"
- "Only Galactic tier cards may be played"

**Boss**: The Dean
- Attack: "Peer Review" — Deals damage based on your highest-tier card
- Defense: "Tenure" — Immune for 1 turn after taking damage

**Strategy**: Keep a diverse deck. Don't rely on single-tier strategies.

---

### Dungeon 2: The Mint of Chains

**Theme**: Financialization, debt, extraction

**Mechanic**: Every action costs HP
- Playing cards: 5 HP
- Drawing cards: 3 HP
- Special abilities: 10 HP

**Boss**: The Creditor
- Attack: "Compound Interest" — Damage increases each turn
- Heal: "Debt Restructuring" — Heals when you play Feed

**Strategy**: Fast kills. The longer it goes, the worse it gets.

---

### Dungeon 3: The Algorithm

**Theme**: Engagement, prediction, manipulation

**Mechanic**: Rotating resistances
- Round 1: Gamma immune
- Round 2: Beta immune
- Round 3: Alpha immune
- (Cycles)

**Boss**: The Feed
- Attack: "Recommendation" — Uses your most-played card type against you
- Special: "A/B Test" — Randomizes its resistances

**Strategy**: Balanced deck with all frequencies. Predict the pattern.

---

### Dungeon 4: The Shadow Archive

**Theme**: Projection, hidden selves, duality

**Mechanic**: Your deck is mirrored
- The System plays copies of your cards
- Your strengths become its strengths

**Boss**: The Double
- Attack: "Reflection" — Deals damage equal to your highest damage dealt
- Counter: "Projection" — Redirects debuffs back to you

**Strategy**: Build a deck with self-synergy, not raw power.

---

### Dungeon 5: The Five Scrolls

**Theme**: Narrative control, story, mythology

**Mechanic**: Elemental phases
- Fire phase: Attack cards deal double damage
- Water phase: Heal cards doubly effective
- Earth phase: Defense cards provide extra shield
- Air phase: Speed determines everything
- Aether phase: All curses are doubled

**Boss**: The Storyteller
- Changes phase every 2 turns
- Immune to non-matching elements

**Strategy**: Carry cards for all elements. Swap based on phase.

---

### Dungeon 6: The Bio-Laboratory

**Theme**: Optimization, quantification, reduction

**Mechanic**: Stat tracking penalties
- Highest stat: Reduced by 20%
- Lowest stat: Increased by 20%
- Forces average builds

**Boss**: The Optimizer
- Attack: "KPI Strike" — Targets your highest stat
- Heal: "Efficiency" — Gains HP when you waste actions

**Strategy**: Even stat distribution. Don't min-max.

---

### Dungeon 7: Transmission (Final Boss)

**Theme**: The System itself

**Mechanic**: Uses YOUR minted cards
- Every card you've ever minted appears in its deck
- Your rarest cards become its weapons

**Boss**: The Signal
- Phase 1: Tests your fundamentals
- Phase 2: Uses your Common cards
- Phase 3: Uses your Rare cards
- Phase 4: Uses your Legendary cards
- Phase 5: Uses The Inversion (auto-win if you have it)

**Strategy**: This is the ultimate inversion. The deck that defeats you is your own.

## Advanced Strategies

### The Inversion Strategy

Purposefully build a "weak" deck that exploits The System's expectations:

1. Use low-power, high-curse cards
2. Let The System underestimate you
3. Activate curse-synergy effects
4. Win through unexpected combinations

### The Shadow Deck

Embrace The Shadow Archive mechanic:

1. Build a deck that counters itself
2. When mirrored, you know exactly how to beat it
3. The System plays into your trap

### The Frequency Lock

Coordinate Cell frequencies:

1. Each player specializes in one frequency
2. Combined, you cover all resonances
3. Enemy cannot counter all simultaneously

### The Bullet Gift Gambit

Use TradingPost strategically:

1. Gift powerful cards to Cell members before battle
2. They use them against the enemy
3. You maintain deck consistency
4. Strengthen the whole Cell

### The Etymology Edge

Understanding WYRD word origins reveals hidden mechanics:

- **Mort** (death) cards: Stronger when HP is low
- **Vita** (life) cards: Heal more when HP is full
- **Lux** (light) cards: Reveal hidden enemy stats
