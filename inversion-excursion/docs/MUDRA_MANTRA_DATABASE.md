## 1. THE TEN MUDRĀS OF HAṬHA YOGA PRADĪPIKA

### 1.1 MAHĀ MUDRĀ (The Great Seal)
**Sanskrit:** महा मुद्रा
**Classification:** Foundation Mudrā

**Formation:**
1. Sit with left heel pressing the perineum (yoni-sthāna/mūla-sthāna)
2. Extend right leg, grasp right toes with right hand
3. Apply Jālandhara Bandha (chin lock)
4. Inhale deeply, drawing prāṇa downward
5. Hold breath, visualizing energy at root
6. Exhale slowly
7. Practice equally on both sides

**Effects:**
- "Destroys great evils and pains, like death"
- "Neutralizes effects of all rasas" (poisons)
- "Even the deadliest of poisons, if taken, acts like nectar"
- Cures: consumption, leprosy, prolapsus ani, colic, indigestion
- Awakens Kuṇḍalinī, directs prāṇa into Suṣumnā

**WYRD Etymology:**
- **Mahā** (Great): From *mah* (to measure, to honor). That which is measured as supreme.
- **Mudrā** (Seal): From *mud* (to rejoice) + *rā* (to give). The gesture that gives joy.

**SynSync Protocol:**
- Primary: 7.83 Hz (Schumann Resonance)
- Secondary: 396 Hz (Root Liberation)
- Duration: 10-15 minutes
- Position: Seated, spine erect

**In-Game Application:**
- **Dungeon:** Ivory Tower
- **Function:** "Descent" ability—move downward through hierarchy
- **Boss:** The Pedant—neutralizes credential-based attacks
- **Mechanic:** Grounding, foundation, reality anchor

### 1.2 MAHĀ BANDHA (The Great Lock)
**Sanskrit:** महा बन्ध
**Classification:** Bandha (Lock)

**Formation:**
1. Left heel to perineum; right foot on left thigh
2. Apply Jālandhara (throat lock) or press tongue to upper teeth
3. Inhale, apply locks, fix mind in Suṣumnā or between eyebrows
4. Hold until prāṇa stabilizes
5. Exhale slowly
6. Practice on both sides

**Effects:**
- Stops upward motion of nāḍīs
- Joins Triveṇī (Idā, Piṅgalā, Suṣumnā)
- Carries mind to Kedāra (between eyebrows)
- "Most skilful means for cutting away the snares of death"

**WYRD Etymology:**
- **Bandha** (Lock): From *bandh* (to bind). The paradox: we bind to liberate.

**SynSync Protocol:**
- Primary: 10 Hz (Alpha coherence)
- Secondary: 432 Hz (Universal harmony)
- Duration: 5-10 minutes

**In-Game Application:**
- **Dungeon:** Ivory Tower (secondary)
- **Function:** Energy binding, focus lock
- **Mechanic:** Prevents energy drain, maintains state
