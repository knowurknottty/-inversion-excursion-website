### Sacred Text Etymologies

**Bible** (Greek *biblia* "books"): From *bublos* (papyrus), from Phoenician city Byblos. The container becomes the content.

**Veda** (Sanskrit "knowledge"): From *vid-* (to know, PIE *weid-). Direct seeing.

**Sutra** (Sanskrit "thread, string"): From *siv-* (to sew). The thread that binds teachings.

**Tantra** (Sanskrit "loom, weave"): From *tan-* (to stretch). The extended system.

**Quran** (Arabic "recitation"): From *qara'a* (to read). The spoken becoming law.
