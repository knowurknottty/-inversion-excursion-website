# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about The Inversion Excursion.

---

## General Questions

### What is The Inversion Excursion?

The Inversion Excursion is a Farcaster-integrated blockchain card game where you collect Scroll Cards, build decks, and fight The System's dungeons together with other players (Cells). It combines brainwave entrainment (SynSync), NFT minting, and cooperative gameplay.

### Is it free to play?

Yes! The core game is free:
- Starter cards are free
- Joining Cells is free
- Battling dungeons is free

Optional purchases:
- Minting victory screenshots (gas fees)
- Trading card gifts (gas fees)
- Premium card packs

### What do I need to play?

1. A Farcaster account
2. A crypto wallet (Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
3. Some ETH on Base network (for transactions)
4. Headphones (for SynSync protocols)

### Which blockchain does it use?

Base (Coinbase's Layer 2). It's fast, cheap, and Farcaster-native.

## Gameplay Questions

### How do I win?

- Form a Cell (3-7 players)
- Build a balanced deck
- Run SynSync protocols for bonuses
- Defeat dungeon bosses together
- Survive 10 rounds + kill the boss

### What's a Cell?

A Cell is your squad — other players you battle with. Think guilds or parties in other games. You share a "resonance field" that combines your cards' frequencies.

### Can I play alone?

Yes, but it's harder. Some dungeons require Cells. Solo play is good for learning, but the game is designed for cooperation.

### What are the Seven Dungeons?

1. **The Ivory Tower** — Credentials and gatekeeping
2. **The Mint of Chains** — Financialization mechanics
3. **The Algorithm** — Rotating resistances
4. **The Shadow Archive** — Your deck mirrored against you
5. **The Five Scrolls** — Elemental phases
6. **The Bio-Laboratory** — Stat optimization
7. **Transmission** — Final boss using YOUR cards

### What happens if I lose?

- You don't lose cards
- Learn from the battle log
- Adjust your deck
- Try a different strategy
- The System teaches through failure

## Card Questions

### How do I get cards?

1. **Starter pack** — Free when you join
2. **Dungeon drops** — Rewards from victories
3. **Minting** — From SynSync screenshots
4. **Gifts** — Other players can gift you cards
5. **Trading** — One-way gifts through TradingPost

### What makes cards different?

Each card has:
- **Power** — Attack damage
- **Defense** — HP and protection
- **Speed** — Turn order
- **Frequency** — Hz value for resonance
- **Curse** — Negative effect when played

### What's a curse?

Every powerful card has a cost. Examples:
- The Pedant: +3 Intellect, -1 Speed
- The Golem: +5 Defense, can't retreat
- The Puppeteer: Control enemy, lose 2 cards

The curse IS the game. Master the inversion.

### Can I sell my cards?

No. Cards are soulbound by default. You can only:
- Gift them (one-way, no take-backs)
- Keep them forever

This aligns with the book's "Bullet Gift" philosophy.

### What's the rarest card?

**Cosmic tier** (0.5% drop rate). These have:
- 70-100 stats
- Unique animations
- Ethereal visual effects
- Transformative curse mechanics

### How many cards can I have?

- **Deck**: 8-20 cards
- **Inventory**: Unlimited
- **Duplicates**: Max 2 of any card in deck

## SynSync Questions

### What is SynSync?

SynSync is brainwave entrainment — using sound to guide your brain into specific states (focus, relaxation, creativity, etc.)

### Do I have to use SynSync?

No, but it helps. Running a protocol before battle gives bonuses:
- Faster card draw
- Reduced curse effects
- Damage boosts
- Special abilities

### How does it work?

1. Choose a protocol (Alpha, Theta, Gamma, etc.)
2. Put on headphones
3. Listen for 60 seconds
4. The game detects genuine entrainment
5. Get battle bonuses

### Is it safe?

Yes. SynSync uses standard binaural beat technology:
- No electrical stimulation
- Just audio through headphones
- Used in research and therapy for decades

### What if I can't entrain?

That's okay:
- Try different frequencies
- Ensure quiet environment
- Some people entrain easier than others
- Bonuses are helpful but not required

### Which protocol should I use?

| Protocol | Effect | Best For |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **Gamma** | +20% attack speed | Aggressive decks |
| **Beta** | See enemy early | Strategic play |
| **Alpha** | +15% healing | Defensive decks |
| **Theta** | Random free card | High variance |
| **Delta** | +30% defense | Tank builds |
| **Schumann** | Cell-wide buff | Team coordination |
| **432 Hz** | Secret effects | Rare discoveries |

## Cell Questions

### How do I join a Cell?

1. Get an invite code from a Cell member
2. Or browse open Cells in the game
3. Click "Join Cell"
4. Start battling together

### Can I be in multiple Cells?

No. One Cell at a time. Leave your current Cell to join another.

### What does a Cell Leader do?

- Invite/kick members
- Start battles
- Set Cell emblem
- Call strategy during battles

### How many people should be in a Cell?

3-7 players. More players = more powerful resonance field, but harder to coordinate.

### What if my Cell is inactive?

- Message them on Farcaster
- Leave and find a new Cell
- Create your own Cell
- Check "active now" status before joining

## Technical Questions

### What wallet should I use?

Recommended:
- **Rabby** — Best for power users
- **Coinbase Wallet** — Good for beginners
- **Rainbow** — Mobile-friendly

Any wallet that supports Base network works.

### How much ETH do I need?

For testing: 0.001 ETH (faucet available)
For playing: 0.01 ETH should last weeks
Minting victories: ~0.0001 ETH per mint

### Is my data safe?

Yes:
- Wallet signatures, not passwords
- No email required
- No personal data collected
- On-chain data is public (like all blockchain)

### Can I play on mobile?

Yes, through:
- Mobile browser
- Farcaster app (Frame integration)
- Wallet app browsers

Best experience is desktop + headphones for SynSync.

### What if the game goes down?

Your cards are NFTs on Base — you always own them. The game is just an interface. Even if servers go down, your assets are safe on-chain.

## Minting Questions

### How do I mint a card?

1. Complete a dungeon
2. Take a screenshot
3. Choose "Mint as Card"
4. Card stats generated from screenshot
5. Pay gas fee
6. Card appears in your inventory

### What determines card stats?

- **Screenshot content** — Different dungeons = different stats
- **SynSync protocol used** — Frequencies affect card Hz
- **Rarity roll** — Random tier (Common to Mythic)
- **Dungeon difficulty** — Harder dungeons = better cards

### What are victory NFTs?

Screenshots of your victories, minted as commemorative NFTs. These are:
- Soulbound (can't transfer)
- Proof of achievement
- Shareable on Farcaster
- Visual record of your journey

### Why mint victories?

- Bragging rights
- Permanent record
- Share with Farcaster friends
- Some victories unlock special content
- Support the game (optional fees)

## Troubleshooting

### "Transaction failed"

- Check you have enough ETH
- Try again (network congestion)
- Increase gas limit slightly
- Contact support if persistent

### "Can't connect wallet"

- Ensure wallet supports Base network
- Add Base manually if needed
- Network ID: 8453
- RPC: https://mainnet.base.org

### "SynSync not detecting"

- Use headphones (required)
- Turn up volume
- Quiet environment
- Try different protocol
- Check browser permissions

### "Cell invite not working"

- Code may have expired
- Cell may be full (max 7)
- You may be in another Cell
- Ask leader for new code

## Strategy Questions

### Best deck for beginners?

**The Balanced Starter**:
- 2x Golem (defense)
- 2x Pedant (intel)
- 2x Alchemist (heal)
- 2x Feed (card draw)

Simple, forgiving, teaches mechanics.

### How do I beat The Shadow Archive?

Your deck is mirrored, so:
- Build self-synergistic decks
- Avoid raw power (enemy gets it too)
- Include counter-cards to your own strategy
- Think "what would beat my deck?"

### What's "The Inversion" combo?

5+ cards with the same frequency:
- Instantly wins the battle
- Burns all cards (they're destroyed)
- High risk, ultimate reward
- Requires perfect deck building

### How important is Cell coordination?

Very:
- Share frequency coverage
- Call out enemy intentions
- Coordinate curses (some help allies)
- Time healing together

Voice chat or Farcaster DMs recommended.
