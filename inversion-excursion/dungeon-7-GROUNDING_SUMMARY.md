## Core Research Findings Applied

### 1. ARG Mechanics Connection
**Finding**: QAnon deliberately borrows from Alternate Reality Games
- TINAG ("This Is Not A Game") slogan appropriation
- Community puzzle-solving ("baking")
- Apophenia as gameplay mechanic
- Playframe misalignment exploited

**Dungeon Integration**: The Labyrinth's "red string" mechanic mirrors the obsessive pattern-searching of ARG players.

### 2. Echo Chambers vs. Bubbles
**Finding**: Nguyen's distinction explains why facts don't work
- Bubbles = not hearing other views (easily fixed)
- Chambers = hearing but *discrediting* other views (cult-like, robust)
- QAnon operates as echo chamber

**Dungeon Integration**: The "Identity-Protector" dialogue demonstrates the discrediting mechanism.

### 3. Failed Prophecy Pattern
**Finding**: Festinger's cognitive dissonance research
- Failed predictions → intensified commitment, not abandonment
- Historical pattern: Millerites, Branch Davidians, Heaven's Gate
- QAnon: "The Storm" repeatedly postponed, believers adapt

**Dungeon Integration**: The "Unraveling" section addresses the grief of admitting error—mirroring the psychological pain Festinger documented.

### 4. Confirmation Bias & Motivated Reasoning
**Finding**: Stanford death penalty study (Lord et al., 1979)
- Same evidence interpreted oppositely by opposing groups
- Motivated reasoning: scrutiny scales inversely with preference
- Backfire effect: corrections sometimes strengthen beliefs

**Dungeon Integration**: The Gyan Mudrā practice—returning to direct evidence—counteracts these biases.

### 5. Radicalization Pipeline
**Finding**: 3N Model (Need, Narrative, Network) and 5-stage process
1. Personal grievance
2. Identity fusion
3. Epistemic isolation
4. Dehumanization
5. Violence justification

**Dungeon Integration**: The "Descent Into Pattern" maps this progression from legitimate skepticism to totalizing belief.

### 6. Cult Tactics Checklist
**Finding**: Standard cult manipulation techniques documented
- Love bombing, thought reform, guilt trips
- Information control, private language
- Induced phobias, promises of exclusive salvation
- All present in QAnon

**Dungeon Integration**: The "Architecture of Suspicion" mirrors cult indoctrination structures.
