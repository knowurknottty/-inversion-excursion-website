# Inversion Excursion

**"The ultimate journey to remember who is watching."**

## Overview
"Inversion Excursion" is an epic action-adventure RPG inspired by the classic mechanics of *The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past*, reimagined with PS5-level visual fidelity and a profound philosophical narrative.

The game explores the "masterful deceptions along the course of human evolution," leading the player from a state of being "fully plugged into the system" to a full spiritual awakening where they realize the true nature of the Observer.

## Core Pillars
- **The Great Inversion:** Uncovering the systematic deceptions of modern society (Education, Finance, Language, Religion).
- **The Awakening:** Transitioning from the physical/material deceptions to the spiritual/metaphysical truths (Biofields, Resonance, The Hologram).
- **The Three Scrolls of Truth:**
    - **I AM**
    - **YOU ARE**
    - **IT IS**
- **The Observer:** Realizing the identity of the entity experiencing the avatar.

## Visual & Audio Style
- **Zelda 3 Aesthetic, PS5 Fidelity:** High-resolution textures and modern lighting applied to a classic top-down perspective.
- **Epic Orchestral & Synchronic Audio:** Music that evolves with the player's level of consciousness.

## Project Structure
- `docs/`: Game Design Documents, World Building, and Narrative outlines.
- `src/`: Initial prototype code and logic.
- `assets/`: Concept art, UI mockups, and sound design notes.
