## The Architecture of Reduction

**The Waiting Room:** You sit with others who have "symptoms." Each has a number. Each has a file. The file contains everything about you except who you are.

**The Examination:** You are weighed, measured, scanned, sampled. Your blood is taken. Your urine is analyzed. Your genetic code is sequenced. You become data. The data becomes the reality. If the data says you are healthy, you are healthy—even if you feel like dying. If the data says you are sick, you are sick—even if you feel fine.

**The Diagnosis:** A category is assigned. The category has a code. The code determines treatment. The treatment has side effects. The side effects require more treatment. You enter the system with a complaint and become a customer for life.

**The Experiment:** You are told you are being "treated," but you are actually being experimented upon. The treatments change. The protocols shift. The goal is not your health—it is the accumulation of data, the refinement of control, the maintenance of the system.
