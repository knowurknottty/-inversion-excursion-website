# EPWORLD Marketing Copy Audit
## Securities Law Compliance: Investment Language Removal

**Audit Date:** March 7, 2026
**Purpose:** Remove all investment-oriented language and replace with consumptive utility messaging to address Howey Test concerns.

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Critical Changes Required

| Risk Level | Finding | Action |
|------------|---------|--------|
| 🔴 HIGH | Investment-oriented language throughout | Complete rewrite required |
| 🔴 HIGH | Speculation language in tokenomics | Replace with utility focus |
| 🟡 MEDIUM | Price/prediction language in docs | Remove all forward-looking statements |
| 🟡 MEDIUM | Yield/farming terminology | Replace with skill-based reward language |
| 🟢 LOW | Minor "investment" references | Standardized replacement |

### Language Philosophy Shift

**FROM:** "Invest in EPWORLD tokens for passive income and price appreciation"
**TO:** "Use EPWORLD tokens for gameplay, validation, and research bounties"

## CONCLUSION

This audit identifies and replaces all investment-oriented 
language with consumptive utility messaging. By emphasizing:

1. **Immediate gameplay utility** (not future value)
2. **Skill-based rewards** (not passive returns)
3. **Consumptive use** (not speculation)
4. **Active participation** (not token holding)

EPWORLD positions the $EPWORLD token as a functional utility 
token rather than an investment vehicle.

**Remember:** Every piece of external communication must include 
appropriate disclaimers. When in doubt, emphasize utility over 
value.
