## 1. TABLE: All Neuroscience Claims with Verification Status

### A. BINAURAL BEAT ENTRAINMENT

| Claim | Status | Evidence | Citation |
|-------|--------|----------|----------|
| Binaural beats can induce frequency-following response (FFR) | **REPLICATED** | Multiple EEG studies confirm brainwave entrainment at beat frequency | Oster (1973); Ross & Lopez (2020) - 40Hz BB enhanced attention training |
| Binaural beats reduce anxiety | **REPLICATED** | Meta-analysis supports anxiolytic effects in pre-operative patients | Systematic review: 13 studies analyzed, 5 supported entrainment hypothesis |
| Binaural beats improve attention/cognition | **REPLICATED** | 40Hz BB significantly improved attention blink task performance | Ross & Lopez (2020), Sci Rep |

| BB work without headphones | **INCORRECT** | Requires stereo separation for binaural integration in superior olivary complex | Basic auditory neuroscience |

   - Reason: Not derived from peer-reviewed neuroscience; esoteric attributions

   - Reason: No molecular mechanism; no peer-reviewed evidence

3. **Pineal gland activation via sound** (especially 963 Hz)
   - Reason: Zero peer-reviewed studies; biophysical implausibility

4. **Biofield as "template" or carrier of psychological states**
   - Reason: Theoretical framework; not empirically tested

5. **Chakra-frequency correspondences**
   - Reason: Esoteric tradition; no neuroscientific basis

1. **Isochronic tones superiority over binaural beats**

   - Emission is replicated; communication role is debated

3. **Heart EM field emotional information transfer**
   - HeartMath studies; limited independent replication

4. **Schumann resonance direct brain effects**
   - HRV synchronization replicated; brain entrainment contested

### `[Replicated]` Tags for Credibility:
