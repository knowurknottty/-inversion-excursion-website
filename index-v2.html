The Sacred Mirrors

Just as Alex Grey's Sacred Mirrors series reveals the layers of human existence — from physical body to spiritual essence — this book unfolds as seven mirrors, each reflecting a different frequency of consciousness.

✦ The Seven Layers of Perception

  • Physical (Root) — The material world, the body, survival
  • Etheric (Sacral) — The energy body, vitality, life force
  • Astral (Solar) — Emotions, desires, the dreaming mind
  • Mental (Heart) — Thoughts, beliefs, the rational mind
  • Causal (Throat) — Will, intention, creative expression
  • Buddhic (Third Eye) — Intuition, direct knowing, insight
  • Atmic (Crown) — Unity consciousness, divine connection

Each chapter of this book corresponds to one of these layers, offering practices and perspectives to awaken and harmonize that frequency within you.
