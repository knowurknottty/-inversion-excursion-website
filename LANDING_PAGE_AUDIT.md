## WHAT NEEDS IMPROVEMENT

### 1. The Hero Section

**Current:**
- "Your Brain. Your Rules." — Good, but generic
- 81+ Protocols, 100+ Citations — Numbers are buried

**Problem:** The most compelling stats are below the fold

**Fix:** Lead with the 2-minute claim. That's your differentiator.

### 2. The "Why We Built This" Section

**Current:** Long paragraphs about pharmaceutical companies

**Problem:** Preaching to the choir. People who landed here already agree.

**Fix:** Cut by 50%. Keep the punchlines, lose the exposition.

### 3. The Mechanism Section

**Current:** Excellent frequency breakdown

**Problem:** "Evidence Level I" is mentioned but not explained

**Fix:** Add a tooltip or link explaining evidence levels

### 4. The Numbers Section

**Current:** 80%, 40%, 50%, 25%, 84 studies

**Problem:** These are impressive but disconnected from user outcomes

**Fix:** Connect each stat to a specific protocol users can try

### 5. The CTA

**Current:** "Start Your First Session Tonight"

**Problem:** Generic. Doesn't reinforce the 2-minute claim.

**Fix:** "Feel Different in 2 Minutes — Try It Now"

### 6. Missing Elements

- **Protocol preview** — No audio sample
- **Visual proof** — No EEG screenshots, no before/after
- **Trust badges** — No GitHub stars, no community size
- **Urgency** — No reason to act now vs. later
- **Social proof** — No "X people used this today"

## TECHNICAL IMPROVEMENTS

### Performance
- **Lazy load** images below fold
- **Preload** critical assets
- **Minimize** JavaScript (currently ~200KB)
- **Compress** images (WebP format)

### SEO
- **Meta description:** "118 brainwave protocols for anxiety, sleep, focus. Effects in 2 minutes. Open source, zero tracking, free forever."
- **Open Graph:** Custom image with stats
- **Structured data:** FAQ schema, Product schema

### Accessibility
- **Alt text** for all images
- **Keyboard navigation** for all interactive elements
- **Color contrast** WCAG AA compliant
- **Reduced motion** media query support

### Conversion
- **Exit intent** popup: "Wait — try the anxiety protocol first (2 minutes)"
- **Scroll trigger:** "X people started a session today" (social proof)
- **Email capture:** "Get new protocols first" (newsletter signup)
